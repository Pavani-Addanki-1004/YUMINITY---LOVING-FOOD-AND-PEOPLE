Ingredients

  • 500g Paneer (cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate Paneer with yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook Paneer pieces in a pan until slightly browned, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter and saut√© onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Stir in tomato puree, cumin, garam masala, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Add cream, salt, and cooked Paneer. Let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
