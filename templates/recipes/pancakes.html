Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup and fruits for topping

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; stir until just mixed (donâ€™t overmix).
  4. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat and lightly grease with butter or oil.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup, butter, or fresh fruits.
