Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice (cooked and seasoned with rice vinegar)
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber, cut into thin strips
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 200g fresh salmon or imitation crab sticks
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Pickled ginger and wasabi (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place a bamboo sushi mat on a flat surface and put a sheet of nori on top (shiny side down).
  2. Spread an even layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about 1 inch uncovered at the top edge.
  3. Arrange cucumber, avocado, and salmon/crab in a line across the center of the rice.
  4. Using the bamboo mat, roll the sushi tightly from the bottom up, pressing gently as you go.
  5. Seal the edge with a little water and slice the roll into 6‚Äì8 even pieces with a sharp knife.
  6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Enjoy!
